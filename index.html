Decentralized Trading Platform

Utility Coin

Capstone Coin is an essential utility coin which will continue to appreciate in value as usage increases in the financial markets through the numerous transaction processes.

Capstone Coin will also be used by clients and traders for deposit and withdrawal purposes. It will also be available for public trading and speculation. Every transaction of Capstone Coin on the blockchain will also generate a fee payout to all hodlers (anyone holding the coin) to encourage the trading of the coin.

The consequence of this will lead to increase in trading volume, thereby ensuring a constant demand and supply.

This will allow the coin to be publicly traded as well as to facilitate deposit and withdrawal processes by clients of Capstone Markets.

What Is Capstone Markets

Capstone Markets is a broker providing Equities, Indices, Commodities, Metals, Forex & Cryptocurrency.

Capstone Markets provides a full range of services including and not limited to Financial Education, Trading Alerts, AI software and Algo Trading Robots.

Traders have a one-stop platform to satisfy all their trading needs.

Capstone Markets has developed an app with ewallet to facilitate deposits/withdrawals.

The app has Trade Alerts sent by push notification to users, high accuracy Trading Platform, Financial News Updates & Analysis and many other features.
